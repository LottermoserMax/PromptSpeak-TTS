Privacy Policy

PromptSpeak: Text to Speech

1. Introduction

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains how the macOS and iOS application PromptSpeak: Text to Speech (“App”, “Service”, “we”, “us”, “our”) handles information. Because the App is fully offline and free of charge, it does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. By using the App you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the terms of this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Data Collection and Use

PromptSpeak operates entirely on your device. The text you enter, your voice settings, and an optional OpenAI API key are saved locally via Apple’s SwiftData framework. The API key, if provided, is secured in the Apple Keychain and never leaves your device. When you tap Generate, the App sends your text over an encrypted HTTPS connection directly to the OpenAI Speech API solely for the few seconds required to create speech. We keep no server logs, copy no text, and receive no metadata that could identify you. The resulting audio file is stored only in the folder you choose (for example, your Desktop); we never upload or back‑up that file.

3. No Purchases, Ads, or Tracking

The App is completely free. It contains no in‑app purchases, subscriptions, advertising frameworks, analytics SDKs, or other tracking technologies. Consequently, we never receive billing information, purchase tokens, or advertising identifiers.

4. Children’s Privacy

PromptSpeak is rated 17 + on the App Store and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so that we can delete it without delay.

5. Data Security

Although no system can guarantee absolute security, we protect your information with industry‑standard measures such as TLS 1.3 for network traffic, Keychain encryption for secrets, and Apple’s hardened runtime. Because we retain no personal data, any request for access, correction, or deletion can be fulfilled by removing the files or uninstalling the App.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or legal requirements. The “Last updated” date will change accordingly, and significant modifications will be announced within the App. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised Policy.

7. Contact

For questions or concerns about this Policy, please e‑mail lottermoser.max@icloud.com.
